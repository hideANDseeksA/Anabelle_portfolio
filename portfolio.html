Activities Insights

"Word Café"

For me, this "Word Café" activity is a very effective way to engage, although it's quite tiring. You only get a few minutes at each station to gather information, which can be a bit challenging. But what's really enjoyable is that all the groups are actively participating, making the experience even more exciting and fun!

Summative test Insights

"For me, this summative test is quite pressuring because there are questions that can be confusing. I often find myself thinking, 'What if I fail?' Even after reviewing, there are still parts of the test with difficult questions. And even though it's multiple choice, it's still really hard to choose the right answer."

Infographics

This activity of creating an infographic was quite challenging for our group because none of us are skilled in editing or design. However, we gave it our best effort, and through this task, we were able to explore and enhance our skills in editing and designing infographics. It was a rewarding experience that pushed us to learn and grow together as a team.

Weak Talk Insights

"Here in Weak Talk, it's so nerve-wracking because you start overthinking whether the number you pick is right, and if the briefcase contains something easy or difficult. It’s that feeling when you’re standing in front, already sweating, but then, just when it’s your turn to answer, the banker calls. And that's the thing with this game – it's all about luck. For me, I was one of the lucky ones that day, and the grade they gave me wasn't bad at all!"
